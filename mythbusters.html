MythBusters

Common Myths Corrected by Scripture

Every card below is one myth. Watch the short video, then read the full study. Video and study stay together so truth is easy to verify and apply.

Myth 1

The Forbidden Fruit Was an Apple

The Lie Genesis says Adam and Eve ate an apple.

The Truth Genesis never names an apple. Scripture speaks only of fruit, and the test was obedience to God's word, not fruit type.

Bible: Gen. 2:17; Gen. 3:1-6

Myth 2

Noah's Ark Is Too Impossible to Be Real

The Lie The ark account is symbolic fiction.

The Truth Genesis records measurable dimensions, and Jesus treats the flood as real history tied to final-day warning.

Bible: Gen. 6:14-16; Matt. 24:37-39; Heb. 11:7

Myth 3

Science Has Disproved the Bible

The Lie Faith and evidence cannot agree.

The Truth Scripture invites investigation and stands up to manuscript, historical, and prophetic testing.

Bible: Isa. 1:18; Job 26:7; Isa. 40:22

Myth 4

The Dead Are Already Conscious in Heaven or Hell

The Lie The dead remain conscious and can communicate with the living.

The Truth Scripture describes death as sleep until resurrection, and resurrection hope is anchored in Christ's return.

Bible: Eccl. 9:5; John 11:11-14; 1 Thess. 4:16-17
